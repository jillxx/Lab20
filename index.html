Hometown

My hometown is the capital of China, the world's most polulous capital city. It has history stretching back 3 millennia. The name "Beijing" means Northern Capital. Beijing is very large and very spread-out. It takes more than 1hr to cross the center city at night.(aka.no trafic.)

Tourist Highlight

As a tourist destination, Beijing has a great deal to offer. Most obvious are the Great Wall, the Forbidden City, The Temple of Heaven, the Summer Palace, Lama Temple, Tiananmen Square, and the traditional courtyard homes along the alleyways or hutongs. A relaxed tour taking in both city and district attractions can eaily fill a couple of weeks.

Foods

Food is a highlight of a visit to Beijing. Specialties include Peking Duck, Mongolian Hotpot, and noodles with meat sauce (zhajiang mian) served up in numerous Old Beijing restaurants.

Weather

Beijing has a very dry climate. June, July and August have higher-humidity and very hot temperatures. December, January and February have average highs just above freezing and accompanied by strong winds.In April, sandstorms sometimes turn Beijing's skies orange and force most people to seek shelter indoors. October generally offers good weather.
